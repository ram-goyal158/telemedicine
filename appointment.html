How to Book an Appointment

Booking your appointment online is easy. Follow these simple steps:

  1. Select your preferred doctor from the dropdown.
  2. Choose a date and time for your appointment.
  3. Fill out your personal details and any additional information.
  4. Click on the "Book Appointment" button to confirm.

Once booked, you will receive a confirmation email with the details of your appointment.
