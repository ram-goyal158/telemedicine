How Follow-up Works

Follow-up appointments are essential for monitoring your health after an initial consultation. Here's how the process works:

  1. Fill out the form with your personal details, appointment ID, and health updates.
  2. Choose a preferred date and time for your follow-up visit.
  3. Submit the form to confirm your follow-up appointment.

Once submitted, you'll receive a confirmation email with the details of your next appointment.

Track Your Health Progress

In your follow-up consultation, doctors will review your health updates and previous appointment notes to monitor your progress and suggest further treatment if needed.
